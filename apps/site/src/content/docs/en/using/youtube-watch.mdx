YouTube Watch lets one participant share a video so everyone in the room watches together.

## Enable the feature

YouTube watch party can be gated behind **Settings → Experimental → YouTube watch party**. Enable it before you expect the share control to appear.

## Share a video

1. Open the **share** / YouTube dialog from meeting controls.
2. Paste a **valid YouTube URL**.
3. The watch **overlay** should play for you and for other participants in the room.

## Playback sync

Play, pause, and seek (as implemented) should **roughly stay in sync** for remote viewers. Exact frame-level lock is not guaranteed on poor networks; small drift can appear.

## Errors and close

| Situation | Expected |
|-----------|----------|
| Invalid URL | Clear error; no blank hang |
| Close watch | Overlay gone for both sides |
| Toggle with stage / whiteboard | Should not permanently break those modes |
